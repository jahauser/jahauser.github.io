About Me

I'm currently a Master's student in the PSI program at the Perimeter Institute, where I'm having the time of my life immersing myself in theoretical physics coursework and exciting research. Fascinated by the dynamics of entanglement in systems at the interface of quantum information and condensed matter, I'm eager to apply both analytical and computational tools to better understand such systems.

Research

Over the past five years, I've conducted research at the Perimeter Institute, Pomona College, TRIUMF, and the University of British Columbia. There, I've investigated topics in condensed matter, gravity, particle physics, quantum information, and quantum optics.
